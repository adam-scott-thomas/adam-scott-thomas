Best pairings

  • White on saturated dark — blue, red, purple, pink, teal
  • Black on saturated bright — yellow, orange, white
  • Stick to one bg per speaker; tint avatars + glow with the same hue, never re-skin the bubble
  • Border = bg lightened ~12% for shape definition without noise
  • Glow = bg @ 30–35% alpha; under 40% to stay calm at small sizes

Avoid

  • Black on navy — looks like dead pixels
  • Dark red / dark purple on black — vibration + low contrast
  • Yellow on white, pink on white, gray on white — washes out at video bitrates
  • Pure blue on pure black — convergent fringing on cheap displays
  • More than 2 saturated speakers per row — fatigues the eye fast
